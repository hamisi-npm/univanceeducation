# CMS Guide — Editors

## Getting started

1. Open Studio at http://localhost:3333 (or the deployed Studio URL).
2. Sign in with your Sanity account.
3. Use the desk sections below to find content.

## Desk sections

### Content

- **Homepage** — hero, sections, featured content
- **About** — page copy + team members
- **Services** — landing page + individual services
- **Destinations** — landing, destinations, universities, partners
- **Blog** — landing, posts, categories, authors
- **Study Guides** — landing, guides, categories
- **Universities** — universities listing page
- **Testimonials** — student stories
- **FAQ** — FAQ page + categories
- **Contact** — contact page copy and form labels
- **Legal** — Privacy Policy and Terms

### Site

- **Site Settings** — brand name, contact, social defaults, global SEO
- **Navigation** — primary menu
- **Footer** — footer columns and links

### People

- Team members and authors (also reachable under About / Blog)

### Taxonomy

- Blog categories, study guide categories, FAQ categories

## Editing tips

- **Singletons** (Homepage, Site Settings, etc.) open as one fixed document — do not create duplicates.
- **Slugs** auto-generate from titles; keep them unique.
- **Images** always need alt text.
- **SEO** fields appear on pages and collection items — fill title and description for search.
- Use **Presentation** (when configured) to preview drafts on the live site.

## Publishing

1. Edit the document.
2. Review validation warnings (required fields, alt text, URLs).
3. Publish. The website updates via Live Content / revalidation.
